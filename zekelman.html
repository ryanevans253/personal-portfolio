ABOUT THE PROJECT

Zekelman, a multi-billion dollar American company, operates many brands in the steel pipe and tube manufacturing space. Wanting to maintain their brand as a modern company, Zekelman hired my employer to manage their brands online presence. When the design work was finished, it was passed to the frontend team to be built out in WordPress.

  • Role

    Senior Frontend Developer

  • Tech Stack

    WordPress | PHP | Pantheon

  • Delivered

    February, 2023

Zekelman About Page

The Approach

My team put together a custom WordPress website that was perfectly to the design spec put together by our talented graphic and UX/UI designers. Building the site in WordPress allowed the client to manage content easily, while still allowing us to develop custom plugins that went far beyond your standard WordPress site.
